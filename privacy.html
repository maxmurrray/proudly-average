Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Proudly Average ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. It's short. We kept it that way on purpose.

What We Collect

When you sign up on our landing page, we collect your phone number. That's it. We don't ask for your name, email, or anything else at this stage.

How We Use It

Your phone number is used exclusively to send you SMS updates about Proudly Average — things like drop announcements, early access, and exclusive offers. We will never spam you. We send messages when there's actually something worth saying.

How We Store It

Your phone number is stored securely in our internal systems. We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. Ever.

SMS Terms

By submitting your phone number, you agree to receive recurring automated text messages from Proudly Average. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies.

To opt out at any time, reply STOP to any message you receive from us. You'll be immediately removed and won't hear from us again unless you re-subscribe. For help, reply HELP.

Cookies

Our landing page does not use tracking cookies or analytics at this time. We're not watching you.

Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed at anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any meaningful changes to this policy, we'll update the date at the top. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated policy.
